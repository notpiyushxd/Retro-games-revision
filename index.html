Pac-Man

Released: 1980 by Namco

Pac-Man is a maze arcade game developed and released by Namco in 1980. The player controls Pac-Man, who must eat all the dots inside an enclosed maze while avoiding four colored ghosts.

Arcade Flashbacks: Did You Know?

  • Golden Era: The late '70s to mid-'80s was the arcade golden age.
  • Pac-Man Fever: Pac-Man generated over $2.5 billion in quarters by the 1990s.
  • Coin Shortage: Space Invaders caused a 100-yen coin shortage in Japan.
  • Kill Screens: Some games had "kill screens," unbeatable levels due to bugs.
  • Atari's Start: Pong (1972) was Atari's first major arcade hit.
  • High Score King: Billy Mitchell was famous for high scores in games like Pac-Man and Donkey Kong.
